This is a sample call script for insurance sales.

GREETING:
- Hello, this is Sarah from TechSolutions Insurance
- How are you doing today?
- Do you have a few minutes to discuss your insurance needs?

INTRODUCTION:
- I'm calling to help you find the best insurance coverage
- We offer comprehensive insurance solutions
- Our rates are competitive and our service is excellent

PITCH:
- Our insurance plans cover all major risks
- We provide 24/7 customer support
- Claims are processed quickly and efficiently
- Special discounts available for new customers

DATA COLLECTION:
- What type of insurance are you looking for?
- What's your current insurance situation?
- What's your budget range?
- When do you need coverage to start?

CLOSING:
- I'll send you a detailed quote within 24 hours
- Would you like to schedule a follow-up call?
- Thank you for your time today
- Have a great day!
