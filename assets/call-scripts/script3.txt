- I understand you might have concerns
- Let me address any questions you might have
- Our service has helped many customers like you

- We're offering a special discount this month
- This offer is only valid for the next 7 days
- You can save up to 30% on your first year

- Would you like to proceed with the premium package?
- I can process your order right now
- Thank you for considering our services
