This is a customer service call script.

GREETING:
- Good morning/afternoon, this is Mike from Customer Support
- Thank you for calling our support line
- How can I assist you today?

PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION:
- Can you describe the issue you're experiencing?
- When did this problem first occur?
- Have you tried any troubleshooting steps?

SOLUTION PROVIDING:
- Let me help you resolve this issue
- I'll walk you through the solution step by step
- Please follow my instructions carefully

VERIFICATION:
- Is the issue resolved now?
- Do you need any additional assistance?
- Is there anything else I can help you with?

CLOSING:
- Thank you for contacting our support team
- Please don't hesitate to call if you need further assistance
- Have a wonderful day!
